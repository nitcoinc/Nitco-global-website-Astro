#### Overview
State and local governments continually seek to add new service categories to their cooperative contract offerings. With evolving technology, there are growing opportunities to improve manual processes, data analytics, and overall efficiency.

#### Challenge
The primary objectives were to reduce service payment authorization processing time and improve data accuracy by minimizing manual data handling.

#### Solution
NITCO provided certified and experienced UiPath RPA developers to create an automated Medicare Service Payment Authorization (SPA) system for a state government agency.

#### Benefits
- Reduced SPA processing time  
- Improved data accuracy by minimizing manual handling  
- Estimated annual savings of over $200,000
