#### Overview  
With the **medical industry** growing rapidly, the **automation of manual tasks** has become a key strategy for improving performance. The industry continually works to **cut costs** and **enhance efficiency** through digital transformation.  

#### Challenge  
The goal was to process **weekly vendor reconciliation requests** within agreed timelines to avoid **cash flow delays** and reduce **manual effort** involved in extracting reports from legacy and web applications. Additionally, automating **manual patient precertification requests** aimed to improve **operational quality** and achieve significant **FTE savings**.  

#### Solution  
NITCO helped the customer **automate manual processes** by assigning a **UiPath robot** to perform vendor reconciliation weekly. Using **easily defined business rules**, the UiPath bot extracted data from **PDFs, legacy, and web applications**, significantly minimizing human error and processing time.  

#### Benefits  
- **100% accuracy rate** in processing  
- **Increased operational quality**  
- **90% FTE savings**  
- **Reduced cycle time** & improved **cash flow**
