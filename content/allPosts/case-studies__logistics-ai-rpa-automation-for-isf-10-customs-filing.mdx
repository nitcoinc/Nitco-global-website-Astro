#### Overview
An international logistics company faced growing compliance risks and operational costs due to manual processing of ISF-10 customs forms for U.S.-bound shipments. With a 10% error rate and potential $5,000 fines per violation, manual inefficiencies endangered speed, accuracy, and compliance—putting timely delivery and cost efficiency at risk.

#### Challenge
The company needed to ensure accurate and timely filing of ISF-10 customs forms. Manual submissions led to a 10% error rate, risking $5,000 penalties for each late or incorrect form, thereby jeopardizing both compliance and operational costs.

#### Solution
NITCO deployed an AI-powered Microsoft Teams chatbot integrated with RPA to automate data collection and ISF-10 form completion. This solution minimized human errors, improved compliance, accelerated transaction times, and introduced a scalable digital workforce to manage customs submissions efficiently.

#### Benefits
- 87% productivity boost  
- 85% employee satisfaction  
- 40% reduction in IT support volume  
- 68% lowered risk of $5,000 violation fines
