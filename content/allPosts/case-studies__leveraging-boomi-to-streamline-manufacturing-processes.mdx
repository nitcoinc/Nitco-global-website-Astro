#### Overview
Manufacturing companies require consistent delivery of goods and services to their customers. With increasing demand and a focus on operational efficiency, scalable technology solutions have become central to driving innovation in this sector.

#### Challenge
NITCO was tasked with implementing the Boomi Integration Platform at the enterprise level and migrating all processes currently running on IBM Sterling Integrator and SSIS (SQL Server Integration Services) to Boomi.

#### Solution
After analyzing the existing setup and identifying areas for improvement, NITCO implemented:
- Boomi High Availability Environments  
- Integration of Airline Carriers and Internal Applications to enhance business value  
- A Publisher/Subscriber (Queue) based solution to decouple dependencies and simplify scaling  
- Boomi EDI/B2B features for seamless EDI Transactions with Trading Partners  

##### Boomi was successfully used to:
- Integrate all 3rd-party carrier APIs with internal applications  
- Build Real-Time and Near Real-Time Integrations between systems  
- Implement EDI Integrations using Boomi’s EDI/B2B capabilities  
- Design a common data model leveraging the Publish/Subscribe model to onboard new systems without disruption  

#### Benefits
- Reduced development time from 6+ months to 4–6 weeks  
- Established a Common Canonical Model used across enterprise applications  
- Saved 80% of development and upgrade costs
