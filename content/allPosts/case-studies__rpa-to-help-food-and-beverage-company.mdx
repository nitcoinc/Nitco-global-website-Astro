#### Overview
Over the years, the food and beverage industry has been expanding rapidly to meet increasing consumer demands, leading to pressure and complexities throughout business processes.

#### Challenge
The customer needed to automate a manual process involving pre-checks such as validating fund transfers, report validation, and uploading data to SAP or other third-party applications. The process also included creating invoices, journal entries, and quarterly performance reports for audits.

#### Solution
NITCO collaborated with the customer’s team to design and develop an enterprise RPA solution using UiPath. The UiPath robot extracted and validated data from SAP and various internal and external web applications to automate the end-to-end process.

#### Benefits
- Significant productivity gains  
- Improved process efficiency  
- Reduced volume of rework  
- Decreased manual effort
