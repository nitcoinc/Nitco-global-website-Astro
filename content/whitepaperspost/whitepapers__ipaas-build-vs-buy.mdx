### A Strategic Framework for Your Integration Future

The decision to build or buy an **iPaaS** solution extends far beyond a simple technology choice. It is a fundamental decision about how your enterprise will allocate resources, manage complexity, and drive future innovation. Building a solution offers maximum customization but requires significant upfront investment, ongoing maintenance, and specialized expertise. Buying a solution accelerates time-to-market and leverages proven technology, but requires careful selection to ensure it meets your unique needs.  

This whitepaper, "iPaaS - Build vs. Buy," simplifies this complex evaluation by focusing on three critical questions every organization must answer. **NITCO** can help you navigate this decision-making process, ensuring you select and implement the best-fit solution that aligns with your long-term strategic objectives.  

### Key Questions to Guide Your Decision

- **What is the Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)?** Explore the hidden costs of building, including development, maintenance, and talent acquisition, versus the predictable costs of a subscription-based iPaaS.  
- **How Quickly Do You Need to Deploy?** Evaluate the speed-to-market advantages of a pre-built platform against the extended development timelines of a custom-built solution.  
- **Can You Support and Scale the Solution?** Assess your internal capacity to maintain, update, and scale a custom platform as your business and technology needs evolve.
