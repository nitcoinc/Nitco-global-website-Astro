### The Limitations of Generic AI Solutions

Many organizations invest in **AI** with high expectations, only to find that generic or siloed solutions create more complexity than they solve. These point solutions often fail to integrate with unique business processes, cannot orchestrate complex enterprise-wide workflows, and lack the essential governance, security, and compliance controls needed for trusted, widespread adoption. This limited scope stifles innovation and prevents AI from delivering on its full transformative potential.  

This whitepaper, "Generic or Siloed AI and Strategic Gaps," explores why a one-size-fits-all approach to AI is insufficient for modern enterprises. It details the necessity of moving toward deeply integrated, **custom AI solutions** that are designed to address your specific operational challenges and strategic objectives.  

### Key Challenges with Generic and Siloed AI

- **Lack of Deep Integration:** Point solutions often fail to connect seamlessly with your existing systems, creating data silos and fragmented workflows that hinder efficiency.  
- **Inability to Orchestrate Complex Processes:** Generic AI struggles to manage and automate the sophisticated, cross-functional processes that are critical to enterprise operations.  
- **Insufficient Governance and Security:** Without robust governance, security, and compliance controls, scaling AI across the enterprise introduces significant risk and undermines trust.  
- **Limited Customization:** Off-the-shelf models are not tailored to your unique workflows, leading to subpar performance and a failure to address core business needs.
