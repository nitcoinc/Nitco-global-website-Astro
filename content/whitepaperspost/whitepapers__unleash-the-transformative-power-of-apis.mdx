Many organizations face a significant challenge: their most valuable data is locked within on-premises applications, and creating the necessary APIs from scratch is too costly and time-consuming. This development bottleneck prevents the creation of compelling, modern applications and hinders digital transformation.  

This eBook, **"Unleash the Transformative Power of APIs and Integration"** by our partner **Jitterbit**, provides a strategic guide to overcoming these obstacles. It explores the history of integration and explains how a modern API integration platform is the key to unlocking enterprise-wide connectivity and innovation.
